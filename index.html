01 / AEROWake Analysis
Wake Analyzer
wake_analyzer.py
Pulls real telemetry to calculate downforce loss when a car runs inside a leading car's aero wake — tracking throttle traces and apex velocities across a reference corner and straight to measure dirty-air instability.
Open Console View source ↗
02 / STRATEGYTire Degradation
Tire Degradation
tire_degradation.py
Processes lateral/longitudinal load over a stint to calculate rubber wear. Accounts for track temperature, slip angle, and traffic gap to forecast the exact lap for a strategic pit stop, with a crisis-override branch for rain.
Open Console View source ↗
03 / DRIVINGGhost Comparison
Ghost Analyzer
ghost_analyzer.py
Overlays a driver's live lap against a pre-loaded perfect "ghost" lap. Using distance interpolation, it flags gaps across braking points, throttle profile, and minimum corner speed — then outputs an engineering directive.
Open Console View source ↗
04 / POWERTRAINERS Deployment
ERS Mapper
ers_mapper.py
Maps hybrid battery state-of-charge across a full lap. Plots harvest thresholds against throttle position to isolate the exact points where MGU-K kinetic recovery switches over to MGU-H thermal deployment, under the 7MJ FIA cap.
